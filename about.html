Michigan Taal is a South Asian fusion dance team that was founded in 2005. The team uses its members` diverse backgrounds in dance to incorporate a variety of styles such as Bollywood, Bhangra, Hip-hop, and Indian Classical into the choreography. Taal is proud of our ever-expanding organization and to be able to meet such talented people from a variety of backgrounds! Want to join? No previous dance experience necessary! We encourage people of all backgrounds to try out, so come and have some fun!
